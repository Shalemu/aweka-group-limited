Central About

AWEKA GROUP LIMITED, based in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania, is a professionally managed car hire and car rental company delivering reliable, efficient, and professional transport solutions to corporate clients, NGOs, institutions, and private individuals. We provide more than vehicles; we offer seamless mobility solutions designed to meet the diverse needs of organizations operating in both urban and remote areas.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted transport partner in Tanzania for NGOs, corporate organizations, and private clients, recognized for excellence, professionalism, and innovative mobility solutions.

Our Mission

To provide safe, reliable, and efficient transport through a modern fleet, professional service, and strong client commitment.
