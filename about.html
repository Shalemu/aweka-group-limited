Company Introduction

Professional Car Hire & Transport Solutions in Tanzania

AWEKA GROUP LIMITED, based in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania, is a professionally managed car hire and car rental company delivering reliable transport solutions to corporate clients, NGOs, institutions, and individuals.

Our modern fleet includes sedans, SUVs, vans, and buses, ensuring reliable mobility solutions for urban and remote operations.

  • Flexible rental packages
  • Corporate & NGO transport
