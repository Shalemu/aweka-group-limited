Our Services

AWEKA GROUP LIMITED provides reliable, efficient, and professional transport solutions
for businesses, NGOs, organizations, and private clients across Tanzania.

Airport Pickup & Dropoff

Reliable, timely, and stress-free transfers to and from airports, ensuring smooth arrivals and departures.

SGR Pickup & Dropoff

Seamless connectivity to Standard Gauge Railway (SGR) stations for intercity travel and field assignments.

Hotel Transfers

Coordinated transfers between hotels, offices, and project sites for efficiency and comfort.

Rapid City Transfers

Flexible and dependable city transport for meetings, workshops, and daily organizational activities.

VIP & Corporate Transport

Professional travel solutions for senior staff, consultants, and dignitaries, ensuring comfort and discretion.

Meet & Greet Services

Personalized reception at airports, SGR stations, or project sites for staff, visitors, and partners.

Hotel Check-in/Check-out Assistance

Assistance with hotel procedures to save time and reduce logistical challenges for staff and partners.

Upcountry Transport

Safe, reliable, long-distance travel for project sites, field visits, and inter-regional operations.

Wedding & Event Transport

Organized and stylish transport for events, workshops, conferences, and community engagements.

Funeral Transport

Group Transport & Shuttle Services
